About me

I have a MSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Coimbra, Portugal. I started my career in 2011 as an IT Trainee in a major Portuguese telecommunications company where I worked in different projects and gained experience with different technologies (mainly HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySql and jQuery Mobile). Then I worked as a Research fellow in University of Coimbra, developing computer vision and image processing algorithms. I am currently a C++ Software Engineer in a startup company, developing real time image enhancement software for medical devices. I participate in all the phases of the software development lifecycle process (definition of software requirements, architectural design, development and testing). I have implemented Scrum methodology with my team. I have been gaining experience with different technologies such as C++, Cuda, OpenGL, OpenCV, GIT and Linux.

I have been improving my Web Development skills on Free Code Camp's Web Development Certification. Most of the projects shown bellow are part of this certification.

I am passionate about developing apps that can make a difference in people's lives.
